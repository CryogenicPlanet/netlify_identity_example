I'M GERALD



I’m Gerald. An entrepreneur, web developer, project manager, one-time blogger, IT professional and support engineer, part-time photographer and videographer, full-time family man. This is my corner of the internet where I post my ravings and share my work. If you want to get to know more about what I’m about, then you found the right place. If you want to work with me, or hire me, then please click the contact me link in the main menu. Welcome to my story.
I’m a Developer. Web development is my passion and professional focus. I work in HTML5, CSS, and Javascript. I’m familiar with jquery, and several other libraries. For front-end development I am most experienced with React + Redux. I have experience working with RESTful API’s, and have created basic API’s using Express.js, Mongoose over MongoDB, and Node.js. I am also currently learning React Native for making mobile apps on both iOS and Android.
I’ve worked for 10+ years as an IT Professional. I currently also work as a support engineer at a VoIP company providing trouble ticket and e-mail based technical support to a large user base of residential and business users. My job entails supporting people that use third party software and hardware that connect to our service, testing new hardware and software, and writing guides and support documentation.
Photography is that thing I do that doesn’t make me any money, but I just can’t stop investing in to it. Luckily, I’ve found ways to integrate photography and videography in to my professional life. I prefer to shoot people because I enjoy telling stories through pictures, and the memories these pictures and videos bring out. I’ve also shot interviews, venues, and events for businesses, friends and family.
While I am an Eagle Scout and enjoy being out in the woods, I’m also a tech entrepreneur. I’ve been part of a couple of startups and like many, I’m hoping one of them sticks. I love nerdy things like comics and gadget. I read sci-fi and fantasy books, as well as biographies and startup stories. I worked at a now defunct technology blog where I was an editor and covered mobile phones and devices. No matter what I do professionally, it will always be about connecting people and technology.
Above all else, I’m a family man. I have an amazing wife, a little boy, and an almost teen-age girl. My world revolves around them, and they drive me to succeed. Family trips and vacations are some of the best times I’ve ever had, and I make sure to give my kids experiences that will help them to grow in to amazing people.
